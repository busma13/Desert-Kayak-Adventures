  • Kayak the Lower Salt River.

    Interested in getting out into nature, but not sure where to start? Let us be your guides! Just show up and we'll take care of the rest. We'll get you out on the water and experiencing the beauty of a desert river, teach you how to be safe on the water, and show you some of our favorite spots.

  • Want a serious adventure?

    If you're past the novice stage, but want someone with experience along for your first trip down some serious rapids, you've come to the right place. We have tour packages on both the Verde River and the Upper Salt River. Enjoy the amazing scenery in between sections of class III and class IV rapids.

Bring your Friends

All tours are a flat rate, so the more friends you bring, the more money you'll save. We can take up to eight people per tour. If you have more friends we can 'make it a double' with two vans, twice the kayaks, and twice the fun.

Choose your Trip

We have everything from half day cruises to multi-day treks, camping out under the stars. Choose the amount of time you have, and how exciting of a river you want to navigate, and will will come up with a list of options that suits your needs.

No Kayak?

Kayak rentals are included with all tours. We have a great selection of sizes and both sit-on and sit-in styles to choose from. If you prefer a paddle board to a kayak then we have you covered there too. We even have giant paddleboards that can sit four people.

Bring the Kids

Children are always welcome on any of our Lower Salt River kayak tours. We have child size kayaks, paddles, life vests, and helmets. Children over age twelve are welcome on our more adventureous tours if they can prove competence prior to our journey.
