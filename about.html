About Us.

We are a small team of outdoors enthusiasts who love to help you get out into nature. We want to get as many people out on a river and enjoying the beauty of Arizona with us.

Meet the crew.

Here are all the wonderful people that make things work around here. They are the best.

Evan Johnstone
Tour guide

Evan is our newest tour guide. He loves paddleboarding and was an avid surfer before moving to Arizona.

Marie Beiler
Tour guide

Marie is an expert kayaker and rafter. She enjoys rock climbing and riding motorcycles in her free time.

Lilian Hamilton
Office Manager, Tour Guide

Lilian runs our office, pays the bills, and keeps everything going smoothly. She guides tours along the lower Salt River.

John Hamilton
Owner, Lead Tour Guide

Jon Hamilton is the owner and lead tour guide for Desert Adventure Tours. He is an avid hiker, kayaker, and mountain biker.
